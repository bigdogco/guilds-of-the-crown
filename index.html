Guilds of the Crown

Guilds of the Crown is a medieval economic simulation inspired by the beloved Guild series. Build your trade empire from a humble workshop, hire and train workers, compete with rival businesses, and navigate the living economy of a growing city. Every market price, every wage negotiation, and every city contract is part of a breathing simulation — your decisions shape the town as much as it shapes you. Currently in active development for PC.
